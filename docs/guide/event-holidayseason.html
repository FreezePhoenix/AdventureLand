Holiday Season on Adventure Land

All New Years drops are global. You can exchange them by finding the right NPC's in Winterland.
Jayson is easy to find.
Faith is inside the Tavern.
You can find Santa with Rudolph to the north of the Arctic Bee's.

You can get a Holiday Spirit buff by interacting with the New Years Tree
If you get the buff after it expires, the tree awards you a fun token!

Collect all 9 pieces of this mystic puzzle and craft the X Box and exchange it from Xyn for something special!

The Holiday Spirit set includes many items, some you can craft, most you get by returning the global drops to the NPC's.

The snowman spawns every hour. Snowman is a collaborative boss, everyone who participates gets a reward. You can only deal 1 damage to Snowman - better participate at each spawn for the common good!

Grinch is a trickster. He portals to you, steals your gold, and escapes before he can be defeated! Make sure you are never alone. Defeating Grinch requires a lot of planning, as he doesn't stay long in one place and can heal himself. When he spawns, he already has 12,000,000+ gold in his pockets!

Dealing 400,000 damage to Grinch unlocks the Festive achievement and a title reward for your equipped cape, so make sure you target him whenever he teleports to you!

Tip: Grinch really hates the sweater!

We also have many new items for the winter and holiday season, like throwable Snowballs!
