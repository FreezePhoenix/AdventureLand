A/B Testing PVP Event

In A/B Testing players are divided into 2 groups to fight. Winning team gets better rewards!

character.team is either "A" or "B" for all participants. The PVP is safe. Unlike other events, at the end of the event, each character is placed back to where they were taken from. In the event of a character reload, you're placed in Mainland. You can only signup in the first 2 minutes, yet you can rejoin to the same team later on if you end up disconnecting.

1000 kills get you the G.achievements.abtesting achievement and the G.titles.abtesting title on your orb! You can see the winner drops at: G.drops.abtesting and loser drops at: G.drops.abtesting_loser
