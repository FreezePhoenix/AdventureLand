Egg Hunt on Adventure Land

Collect all 9 and craft the basket, then exchange it from Xyn for something special!

Collect 4 pieces of the Bunny attire to complete the set and unlock the set bonuses!

Wabbit spawns hourly on a random map and inside a random monster pack. It's super hard to kill for 5 minutes, which should give you enough time to join the fight!

Each time you defeat the Wabbit, you get a 100% Luck buff for 24 hours!

The crafting NPC is near the Goo's in Mainland.
